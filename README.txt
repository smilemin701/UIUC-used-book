CS125 2020SP Final Project

Project Name: UIUC Used Book Trading
Author: Minkyung Chung, Yiwei Liu

The app provides a ground for students to buy and sell books.

Functions: 
- The app requires an Illinois Email to sign up and login, account info are stored online via. Firebase
- Profile tab shows the list of books you put on sale and the status of the book (in stock/sold)
- Buy tab allows you to search books with name, contact the seller via. email with a button click
- Sell tab lets you fill in the form describing what book you want to sell and posts it onto the search list

Features:
- Authentic UIUC coloring templete
- Custom made UI and Icon
